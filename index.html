While the core of Luna Law focuses on divorce and family law, we also represent clients facing criminal charges throughout South Florida. Many situations involving family disputes can intersect with criminal allegations — such as domestic violence claims, DUI arrests, or probation violations. When that happens, you need an attorney who understands both sides of the legal system.

We provide strategic, discreet criminal defense representation designed to protect your record, your freedom, and your future.

DUI / Reckless Driving

A DUI conviction in Florida carries severe consequences including license suspension, fines, and potential incarceration. We challenge breathalyzer accuracy, traffic stop legality, and field sobriety procedures to pursue dismissal or reduction of charges.

Domestic Violence Defense

Domestic violence allegations arise frequently in divorce and custody disputes. False or exaggerated claims can devastate your case and your record. We provide an aggressive, evidence-based defense that protects your rights while navigating the intersection of criminal and family court proceedings.

Drug Charges

From possession to trafficking allegations, Florida drug charges range from misdemeanor to felony and can follow you for life. We scrutinize the legality of the search and seizure, challenge chain of custody, and identify suppression opportunities that prosecutors hope defendants miss.

Theft & Property Crimes

Theft charges in Florida — including petit theft, grand theft, burglary, and fraud — carry penalties that escalate significantly with the value of property involved. We build a thorough defense examining intent, ownership disputes, and the strength of the prosecution's evidence.

Assault & Violent Crimes

Assault, battery, and aggravated assault charges in Florida can result in mandatory minimum sentences. We investigate the circumstances thoroughly, assert self-defense or defense-of-others claims where applicable, and challenge witness credibility and physical evidence.

Probation Violations

A probation violation hearing in Florida does not require proof beyond a reasonable doubt — making swift legal intervention critical. We act quickly to challenge the alleged violation, present mitigating factors, and advocate for alternatives to incarceration before the court issues a warrant.

Hate Crimes

Hate crime enhancements in Florida significantly increase sentencing exposure for underlying offenses. We challenge the government's ability to prove discriminatory motive, scrutinize the evidence presented to support the enhancement, and ensure your constitutional rights are protected throughout the process.

Homicide

Homicide charges — including manslaughter, second-degree murder, and first-degree murder — demand the most rigorous and experienced criminal defense available. We conduct independent investigations, retain expert witnesses, challenge forensic evidence, and develop a comprehensive defense strategy from the earliest stages of prosecution.

Florida follows "equitable distribution" — marital assets and debts are divided fairly, not necessarily 50/50. Courts weigh each spouse's financial situation, contributions to the marriage, length of the marriage, and post-divorce economic circumstances.

Florida courts determine time-sharing based solely on the child's best interests — considering each parent's ability to foster the child's relationship with the other parent, home stability, school and community ties, work schedules, and the child's own preferences when age-appropriate.
